Common Questions

Everything you need to know about shelf tracking.

help How does the expiry notification work?

Shelf-Life uses advanced predictive algorithms and manual input to track your pantry. We send push notifications 48 hours and 24 hours before an item expires, complete with recipe suggestions.

store Can I sync my grocery receipts?

Yes! Our OCR technology supports scanning physical and digital receipts from major Indian retailers like BigBasket, Blinkit, and local supermarkets.

groups Is there a family sharing plan?

Absolutely. The Premium tier allows you to share a single kitchen inventory with up to 5 household members, keeping everyone's grocery list in sync.

lock How secure is my data?

We use enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption for all user data. Your consumption habits are used only to improve your experience and are never sold to third parties.
